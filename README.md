Usage Data: We collect data about your interactions with the app, specifically your pushup logs, dates of activity, and streaks.
Social Data: If you join a group, your Display Name and pushup counts are visible to other members of that group.
Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the Service, such as the device model and operating system, to provide notifications and ensure app stability.
